MY DREAM OF AMBITION

I Have A Dream ….. When I was young, I had a very profound religious experience that lasted with intensity for days, unlike any conceivable earthly feeling. Too be short and to the point, the best way I could describe it was as if I could see a real bright white light in my chest (as crazy as that might sound), accompanied by a very blissful feeling. I had a strong desire to please God, and to spread the teachings of Islam to others, particularly non Muslims. I have not too long ago made a promise to God as I saw something in reach, I said to him, if I ever become encompassed by wealth I will do something very significant in your way and Islam. I had a very ambitious dream, that to my knowledge has never been done. And after my time on earth, I would leave it to a trusted member of the family, and so on, to carry out this grand and overly ambitious vision and let it forever grow and continue to blossom (God willing). InshAllah I will receive the spiritual rewards and mercy of God (in the hereafter) this is the chief reason I truly wish to make this project a reality, thus it giving me a great sense of pride. God willing it will still transpire, even if something much smaller, this is not hard, and if small it surly will be, God willing. When I thought about it then, and still have hopes for it, which was vital to my self assurance and greater happiness. To create something that has never been created, since maybe only in the Golden Age of Islam, and surly in the time of the Prophet (PBUH), something that will impact the country and world in a real and positive way. It is comprised basically of three different branches. First, to make known the true Islam of compassion and universal acceptance of people of different races and faiths, both male and female. Secondly, provide real support and financial assistance for the poor, disabled, sick, physically and mentally ill, and elderly. Thirdly, to establish a Growth Fund for such individuals, to grow indefinitely and infinitely after my time on earth. Having left a great monument in my name, and my families name, to be recognized not only in this world, but more importantly in the heavens. This all by God’s will. The basic organization plan is already drawn out. If it never comes to pass in full, then God willing it assuredly will in even a small part, as having a plan for this as well. But at least having known that I intended it, and created a basic model.

A PERFECT CASUAL DAY WITH A LADY

My ideal start of a day with a gracious lady would be to begin with a nice lunch at Mediterranean café or Asian Bistro. I am definitely indulged by ethnic cuisines and love trying out the newest restaurant on the scene and different exotic regional dishes and pallets. Next, we would head to the movies and catch the latest comedy, sci-fi or horror flick (no slasher movies, don't worry). I really enjoy a good comedy, my favorites are the old school comedies like the National Lampoon’s Vacation movie series. A perfect nights with that special someone would be to dine at an outdoor coastal café at sunset, with the ocean breeze overlooking the horizon with seagulls in flight.
